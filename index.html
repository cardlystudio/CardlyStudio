About Cardly Studio

Cardly Studio is a student‑led creative studio that blends aesthetics with clarity. We design with intention — colours that speak, typography that guides, and layouts that feel effortless. Whether it’s a single card or a full brand identity, we keep it personal, polished and on‑time.

Why clients choose us: customised designs, quick revisions, print‑ready files, and friendly communication.

Working style
1) Share your idea → 2) Get a draft in 24‑48 hrs → 3) Revisions → 4) Final files (PNG, PDF, MP4 as needed). Printing/Delivery can be arranged via partners.
